💙 About Mind

Mind is a UK charity that provides advice and support to empower anyone experiencing a mental health problem. They campaign to improve services, raise awareness, and promote understanding.

With NHS mental health waiting lists getting longer, charities like Mind are more important than ever. Your support helps ensure people can access help when they need it most 💙
